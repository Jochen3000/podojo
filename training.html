Product Dojo Online

Level-up your skills in product discovery, strategy and design

Upcoming Events

Learn how to de-risk and accelerate product innovation

The Product Dojo is a learning journey to practice how to select, combine and apply the right product innovation, strategy and design tools to de-risk and accelerate product innovation.
We start with framing a realistic product innovation challenge and with guidance from product coaches you will then experience the creative flow within a small team from customer discovery to testing product ideas to level up your skills in leading product teams. 
You will leave the Product Dojo with a toolbox of methods from Design Thinking and Design Sprints, Business Model Innovation, Product Discovery and Scrum combined as a blueprint of a Product Innovation Sprint.

Takeaways

  • Learn how to discover, design, test and pitch a compelling value proposition to team members and stakeholders.

  • Practice hands-on Customer Discovery, Design Thinking in a Design Sprint, Job-To-Be-Done, Problem Framing, Value Proposition Design, Story Mapping, Scrum, Testing with no-code tools for rapid prototyping.

  • Update your online collaboration skills and learn new tools for distributed product teamwork.

  • Apply customer and product discovery tools to rapidly discover and design sustainable solutions that fit customer needs.

  • Network with product peers from the product community and experience team dynamics in gamified exercises to improve your leadership style.

  • Leave the Product Dojo equipped with the latest cutting-edge toolbox and ready to design and facilitate your own product innovation sprint.

How we do online sessions

Our sessions are highly immersive and interactive, strictly #noslide learning experiences, not just Zoom calls. You’ll get an in depth online documentation for pre- and post study so that we can focus on highly collaborative teamwork, coaching and knowledge sharing in the live sessions.

We are used to deliver virtual learning, co-creation and product coaching sessions for years. The Product Dojo Online will be run via Zoom, Google Docs and Mural and we will have time to explore more frameworks and formats for online collaboration and use no-code tools for rapid prototyping.

We only teach what we practice when coaching product teams. As experienced product coaches we create a safe space to reveal your leadership skills dealing with complex processes. You will experience team dynamics in a creative flow and you will try out tools in a sequence to be ready to craft your own process flow.
